    What is the goal we're looking to achieve?

    ONU - Goal 13

    SDG 13 intends to take urgent action in order to combat climate change and its impacts. Climate change threatens people with increased flooding, extreme heat, increased food and water scarcity, more diseases, and increased fires and deforestation.

    Introduction

    Climate change has been worse each year. It's caused by human activities and affects life on Earth. With greenhouse gas emissions soearing, climate change has been increasing faster than we thought. We wanted to find a way to try to solve this problem, so we used the classic rule of the 3 Rs. Our project consists of creating a machine that recycles paper. Instead of throwing it away, you can put your paper in this manual machine and recycle it.

  • Procedure

    R3 Paper

    Product:

    First, we started by buying the materials, which were mainly wood, nails, sealant, paint, etc. Then we got together and started putting everything together. We began by cutting our wood to the measurements we needed. Then, we glued the wood to start shaping the base. After that, we put some nails on the glued wood so it wouldn't separate. After shaping it, we started painting all the parts of it. Finally, we began to sand all the uneven parts of the wood. Following this initial phase of construction, we embarked on making a plan to calculate the measurements needed for our project. Then, we procured all the necessary materials. Next, we began to assemble all the pieces and construct the plate's cover, followed by the assembly of the prototype's facade. While constructing, we conducted the final tests for the recycling idea. Once the facade was completed, we proceeded to paint the pieces and assemble the base. Finally, we assembled the water box for drainage.
